Trusted Members

Trusted members take charge and show a willingness to take on other responsibilities and accomplish any task. We want gamers like you to step forward and drive this community. Whether it's spreading the word throughout your community, posting on forums, staying active, giving us ideas for future development, or even keeping an eye on the content of this unique community for gamers, we want you to join us!

VIP MEMBER

When you purchase VIP, you will receive all of the VIP jobs and the permissions to use more tools then the Trusted rank. Also, in paying for VIP you will have the power to vote kick and vote ban users. You can start a vote by typing !menu then go to voting. All donation money goes right back into your gaming community. By supporting Deviant you are helping expand and imporve every aspect!





TeamSpeak

We have a fully built and setup TeamSpeak 3 server, dedicated to our community and gamers. TeamSpeak 3 is a communication network that allows you to stay in contact with members of our community. TeamSpeak 3 also has a mobile app to stay connect while off the computer! More information in the Forums.

Party System

Deviant has a Party System that allows in-game groups to be created to allow private chats, member health and armor HUD, member indications in-game. To start a party type "!party" in-game, to invite members use the GUI or type "!invite". More information in the forums.
